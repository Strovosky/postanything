About Us

Short Reading / Short Writing / Big Practice.

Many times we want to improve our writing in English, but it's hard to know what to write about, where to do it, and specially to find and interesting topic to write about.

Interesting Writing Topics Every Day.

In Post Anything you will have a short interesting topic, question or picture of which you can express your opinion in a post, so you don't have to think every day what you're going to write about.

Keep in mind the idea is that you write a short comment about the topic. People will read your comment, give you some feedback about your grammar, and also post a comment about what you comment.

By practicing reading and writing posts, after a period of 2 to 3 months you're guaranteed to have improved both of those skills.

What if I don't know how to write very well!

Don't worry about that, remember that all of us are here to learn. Still, in your profile you can specify your English level so people can give you feedback according to what your level is.

What type of topics will I write about?.

All of the topics in Post Anything are interesting topics of what is happening at the moment. They can also be thought provoking questions, or a image which you have to consider or describe.
